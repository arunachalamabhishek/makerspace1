Alumni Initiatives

Innovation Endeavours Supported by Alumni 1986 batch of alumni has suppored in setting up Student Innovation Center to promote design & innovation activities among students by providing necessary resources and mentorship. Started in 2011, this endeavour provides three labs meant for fabrication, assembly and ideation for students to use round the clock. The center also provides small seed grants for student innovation activities. 1989 batch of alumni has constituted “IIT Delhi Innovation Award” and seed grants to support student innovation activities. Some of the innovations which have been awarded include: 1. Portable Braille Reader 2. Indoor Navigation System for the Visually Impaired 3. Low Cost Pedal Assisted Electric Tricycle 4. Use of Waste Plastic & Glass in Pavements 5. Hand Held Braille Embosser 6. Pre-Engineered Bamboo Structures for Modern Construction 7. Android App for Women’s Safety 8.Portable Immuno-Magneticcapture System (iMC2) for Early Stage Typhoid Diagnosis 9. Breathe Control Device 10. Building Solutions for Students to Enable Better Access to Learning Resources
