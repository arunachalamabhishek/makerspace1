Medical Device Design

Medical Device Design is a multi-disciplinary one semester course in which student teams are expected to deliver an innovative solution for a clinical need working under the guidance of technology and clinical mentors. The students go through a process of understanding disease state fundamentals of the need being addressed, market analysis and stakeholder analysis. The focus is primarily on identifying need and coming up with solution by immersing in real world environments to capture values and expectations of the target beneficiaries. The students are encouraged to evaluate possible solutions by applying multiple filters including regulatory considerations, markets, Intellectual Property (IP), business and impact.
