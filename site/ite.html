Introduction to Engineering

This is a flagship course offered in the first semester to all 850+ B.Tech. students who are fresh entrants to programme. The purpose of the course is to orient and excite students in the subject of engineering and innovation. The primary objective of this course is to educate students how science and engineering has enormous impact on our day to day lives and how it can be a vehicle for social and economic development. The course enlightens students about the journey of engineering a product/solution as it happens in industry and elsewhere. The course tries to excite students by connecting them with potential role models and successful alumni in engineering profession. Some of the activities which are undertaken as a part of this course include: 1. Thought provoking lectures on exciting topics by successful industrialists, entrepreneurs, designers and alumni. 2. Exposing students to successful research cases, products and innovations which have reached people/industry/society. 3. Experiential learning workshops. 4. Breakout sessions among students in smaller groups to engage in exercises pertaining to design thinking, technology and innovation.
