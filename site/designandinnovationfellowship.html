Design & Innovation Fellowship

Many students are coming forward to stay with the institute beyond their graduation to take their ideas beyond proof of concept. To promote this further IIT Delhi has introduced pre-incubation fellowships for students who would like to continue beyond their graduation up to 12 months for working towards translational research and/or commercialization of their innovations/products. Following students were in residence during 2016-17. 1. Varsha Kumari (Biochemical Engineering & Biotechnology) – Working on process innovation to produce 6 pentyl-α-pyrone (6pp) from renewable sources such as ligno-cellulosic waste. (supported by DIC) 2. Mohammad Asif (Applied Mechanics) – Successfully developed helmet strap tightening mechanism. (Supported by DIC) 3. Pulkit Sapra (Mechanical Engineering) – Working on development of refreshable Braille display device for visually challenged (Supported by Wellcome Trust) 4. Surabhi Yadav (Biochemical engineering & Biotechnology) – Working on development of models of inclusive innovation (Supported by Omidyar Foundation). 5. Mayank Aamseek (Mechanical Engineering) – Worked towards development of tactile books for visually challenged (Supported by MeitY)
